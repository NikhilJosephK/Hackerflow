HackerFlow is SurveySparrow’s first-ever virtual hackathon built to bring together the greatest minds and coding aficionados alike. Engage and collaborate with technology-first individuals to deliver stand-alone web applications that are nothing short of the best!

To all developers, hackathon enthusiasts, and students - this event is for you! Code your way to the top and build apps from scratch using Node.js and React.

Welcome to SurveySparrow’s very own virtual hackathon!

Enough talk. Show us the code.

Hackerflow is SurveySparrow’s first initiative to bring together the brightest minds with the most incredible ideas and to turn them into reality. Form a team, brainstorm & build a web application that reimagines a pain point, or that reinvents a better way to do the same. The platform is open to anyone and everyone across India. You can come as a team of 2-8 or as an individual participant too. Moreover, participation is absolutely free. So what’s stopping you?

Imagine, build, & wow the world. It’s time to break the convention, & refine experiences the better way.
